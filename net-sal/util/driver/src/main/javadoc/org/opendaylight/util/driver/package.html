Specifications for various aspects device modeling and device driver development. These specifications revolve around separation of concerns among the following areas exposed to the consumers of the device driver implementations
  • device information representation ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceInfo} & {@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.Facet})
  • device interactions, i.e. communication & control ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceHandler})
  • device information persistence ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceLoader})
  • device type-based brokering & evolution ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceDriverProvider} & {@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceDriverFactory})
Additionally, the following areas of support are available for the providers of the device driver implementations for various types of devices.
  • device type-specific information interpretation ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceType} & {@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.Facet})
  • device type-based registration, brokering & evolution ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceDriverBroker} & {@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.DeviceDriverFactory})
  • facet-based device information & behaviour presentation ({@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.Facet} & {@link org.opendaylight.util.driver.FacetProvider})
  • number of abstract and default implementations suitable for extension, to ease authoring of device driver implementations
