Representations of OpenFlow Protocol Multipart Message bodies.

The following schematic illustrates the relationship of classes and interfaces that make up a OfmMultipartRequest or OfmMultipartReply. Note that two examples of concrete classes are given:

  • On the left, MBodyDesc: representing the multipart body of a MultipartType.DESC reply, along with its mutable subclass.
  • On the right, MBodyPortDesc: representing a single element of a MultipartType.PORT_DESC reply, along with its inner classes Array (immutable) and Mutable Array.
